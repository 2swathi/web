Product Overview

GENboard is engineered for strength, fire resistance, and moisture tolerance while prioritising environmental safety and responsible end-of-life options. Proprietary binder formulation is excluded from public materials.

Progress & Milestones

  • Lab prototypes tested for density and flexural strength
  • Material optimisation of fiber blends and binders
  • Pilot moulds and finishing trials completed
  • Third-party testing planned

Key Technical Notes

  • Target density: 550–900 kg/m³
  • Surface finishes: smooth, embossed, laminated
  • Standard size: 8 × 4 ft panels
  • Water-based biodegradable binder (confidential)

Compliance & Sustainability

  • Textile waste diversion from landfill
  • Low-energy, formaldehyde-free manufacturing
  • Regional supply chains
  • Non-toxic, zero-VOC indoor use
  • Recyclable or biodegradable end-of-life
