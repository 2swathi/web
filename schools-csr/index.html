Schools & CSR Partnerships

Tardinova Publications works with schools, colleges, and CSR initiatives to support structured innovation and entrepreneurship education through carefully designed learning resources.

For Schools & Colleges

Our publications are designed to integrate smoothly into existing academic environments while supporting experiential, project-based learning.

  • Classroom-ready workbooks and guides
  • Suitable for innovation clubs and enrichment programs
  • Aligned with skill development and future readiness
  • Supports reflection, teamwork, and ethical thinking

Schools may adopt our books as part of:

  • Innovation and entrepreneurship modules
  • Clubs, labs, and after-school programs
  • Short-term workshops or semester-long projects

For CSR & Education Initiatives

Tardinova Publications supports CSR-aligned education programs focused on innovation, sustainability, and entrepreneurship.

  • Book-supported learning programs
  • Structured innovation exposure for students
  • Scalable and measurable educational interventions
  • Long-term learning value beyond one-time events

Our focus is on meaningful educational impact rather than promotional or short-duration activities.

How We Work

  • Understand your educational context and objectives
  • Recommend appropriate publications and formats
  • Support implementation through structured guidance
  • Adapt programs for scale where required

We believe strong programs are built through collaboration, clarity, and shared educational intent.
