About Tardinova Publications

Tardinova Publications is an independent educational publisher dedicated to creating practical, thoughtful learning resources in innovation, sustainability, and entrepreneurship.

What We Publish

We publish books and learning resources that help learners develop structured thinking, real-world problem-solving skills, and ethical awareness.

  • Innovation and design-thinking education
  • Sustainability-aware problem solving
  • Entrepreneurship learning for early founders
  • Reflection-based and experiential learning tools

Our publications are designed to be classroom-ready, facilitator-friendly, and adaptable across learning contexts.

Who We Serve

Our resources are used by:

  • School and college students
  • Educators and facilitators
  • Innovation clubs and learning hubs
  • Early-stage entrepreneurs and founders
  • Educational institutions and programs

Each publication clearly defines its intended audience and learning context.

Our Publishing Philosophy

We believe effective learning goes beyond information delivery. Strong educational resources encourage observation, questioning, iteration, and responsibility.

  • Clear frameworks over jargon
  • Real-world relevance over abstract theory
  • Ethical awareness alongside creativity
  • Learning as a process, not a performance

Looking Ahead

Tardinova Publications is building a growing catalogue of learning resources that support learners across stages — from early innovation thinking to responsible entrepreneurship.

We are committed to publishing materials that remain relevant, adaptable, and grounded in real educational needs.
