About Generative Hub Solutions

Generative Hub Solutions Pvt. Ltd. is a sustainability-focused innovation company transforming waste textiles into next-generation structural materials.

Our flagship product, GENboard, is an engineered composite panel designed for interiors, furniture, and light construction — balancing performance, environmental responsibility, and commercial viability.

Our Focus

  • Circular manufacturing and textile waste diversion
  • Industry partnerships for scalable adoption
  • Research-led materials innovation

Who We Work With
