About Generative Hub Solutions

Generative Hub Solutions Pvt. Ltd. is a sustainability-focused innovation company transforming waste textiles into next-generation structural materials. Our flagship product, GENboard, embodies our mission to make sustainable design commercially viable and accessible across industries.

Sustainability
Circular Design

R&D

Through research, design, and ethical manufacturing, we develop eco-friendly, high-performance boards for interiors, furniture, and building applications — reducing textile waste and promoting circular production systems.

Our Focus

  • Developing sustainable material alternatives to conventional wood-based boards
  • Partnering with designers, builders, and manufacturers for scalable adoption
  • Driving innovation in circular manufacturing through research and collaboration

Who we work with

Architects, interior designers, sustainability consultants, CSR foundations and research institutions.
